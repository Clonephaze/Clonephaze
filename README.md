### 3D Projects

[🎨 ArtStation](https://www.artstation.com/clonephaze) My Art Station account that has a small collection of my work so far with blender and other art softwares.

[📐 3D Printed NFC generator](https://extensions.blender.org/add-ons/nfc-card-keychain-generator/versions/) This add-on lets you design 3D-printable housings for NFC chips. You can add your own logos or icons (Wi-Fi symbol, social media logo, etc.), or use the built-in QR generator to put a backup code directly on the surface of the print.
