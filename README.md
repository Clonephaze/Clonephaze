### Blender Add-ons

[**3D Printed NFC Generator**](https://extensions.blender.org/add-ons/nfc-card-keychain-generator/versions/)  
**Created:** This add-on lets you design 3D-printable housings for NFC chips. You can add your own logos or icons (Wi-Fi symbol, social media logo, etc.), or use the built-in QR generator to put a backup code directly on the surface of the print.

[**Resin2FDM Lite/Advanced**](https://painted4combat.gumroad.com/l/Resin2FdmLite)  
**Maintained:** Resin2FDM is an add-on for Blender 4.0+, designed to aid in converting resin pre-supported miniatures to better suit FDM printing.

[**Blender 3MF Import/Export Format**](https://github.com/Clonephaze/Blender3mfFormat---4.2-compatible)  
**Updated and Maintained:** An add-on for Blender 4.2+ that enables importing and exporting 3MF files. I updated this add-on from Ghostkeeper's work in 2025 after nearly two years without updates, and it now works seamlessly with modern Blender versions.
